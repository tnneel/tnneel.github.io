The Best Homemade Brownies Recipe

Prep time: 10 mins Cook Time: 20 mins Servings: 16

Step 1:

Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F) and grease a baking pan.

Step 2:

Melt ½ cup butter and mix with 1 cup sugar, 2 eggs, and 1 teaspoon vanilla extract. Stir until smooth.

Step 3:

Sift together 1/3 cup cocoa powder, ½ cup flour, ¼ teaspoon baking powder, and a pinch of salt. Add this to the wet mixture and combine.

Step 4:

Pour the batter into the prepared pan and smooth the top.

Step 5:

Bake for 20-25 minutes, until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out with a few moist crumbs.
